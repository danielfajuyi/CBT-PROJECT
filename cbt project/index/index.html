INTRODUCTION

The NIGERIAN MILITARY SCHOOL selection board common entrance examination, to be written by candidates who are aspiring to gain admission into the school, is an examination which questions have been carefully selected and evaluated by respective teachers of the NIGERIAN MILITARY SCHOOL ZARIA. The online compedium is aimed at aquinting candidates with "most likely to see" questions and past questions to further prepare them against the common entrance examination and to improve their chances of being called for the selection board interview
more so during the selection board interview candidates are expected to undergo another examination which includes Quantitative reasoning, verbal reasoning, Mathematics, english language and General knowlwedge. The online compedium has a learning and practice system which is designed to further prepare candidates throughout their screening process.
